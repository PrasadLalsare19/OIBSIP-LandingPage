Welcome to the basic landing page of cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game where two teams compete against each other, each consist of 11 players Cricket is not just a sport but a passion for millions of fans worldwide. It has a rich history and culture, with many legendary players who have left a lasting impact on the game. The sport continues to evolve, and new talents emerge regularly, keeping cricket enthusiasts engaged and excited.

Test Cricket

Test cricket is the oldest and longest format of the game of cricket. It is considered the purest form of cricket and is played over five days.

One Day Internationals(ODI's)

An ODI match is completed in a single day, with each team having 50 overs to bat and bowl.

T20 International(T20I)

Twenty20 or T20I (T20 International), is the shortest format of the game of cricket of 20 overs.
